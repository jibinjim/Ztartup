    About Us

    Ztartup Dubai's first private sector accelerator. Backed by the promoters of Mµrgency & Startup Village, Ztartup Incubator offers incubation and market access support for tech startups. Programs are best suited for startups operating out of Dubai or are looking to grow into the GCC markets.

    Network

    The Ztartup startup and founder network boasts of some of the brightest tech startup founders from the Indian & Dubai startup ecosystems and some of the brigtest minds from the Dubai professional world.

    Incubator

    The incubator program handholds you through the early stages of setting up your business in Dubai including trade license, bank accounts, visa processing and right through commencing business operations.

    Accelerator

    Market access program is a focussed accelerator program for the more advanced startups in the network. This program is targeted at achieving business growth and sales growth for participating startups.
